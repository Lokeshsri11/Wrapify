About Us

About Wrapify Media

Founded in November 2022 by Ayushh Tyagi, Wrapify Media has quickly established itself as a leading social media marketing agency for car detailers. Our journey began with a small, dedicated team of 5, and in just over a year, we’ve expanded to a dynamic group of over 25 professionals.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative and effective marketing solutions that drive brand growth, engagement, and success in the car detailing industry.

Our Journey

Starting with a focus on the USA market, we have successfully expanded our services to India, Canada, and Dubai. Today, we collaborate with 18 distinguished brands, managing over 5 lakhs in ad spend, generating 2500+ leads, and achieving a 15-20% conversion rate.
